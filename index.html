About Samya

Samya is a technology company dedicated to creating AI-powered applications and B2B platforms using 100% open-source software.

Our goal is to make advanced technology accessible, transparent, and meaningful — ensuring that innovation serves people, not the other way around.

We believe in building products that enhance human potential while maintaining ethical, responsible AI practices.

Open-Source Philosophy

Why Open Source?

At Samya, freedom, transparency, and community collaboration are core values. We use and contribute to open-source software to ensure:

  • Zero vendor lock-in
  • Ethical and transparent systems
  • Privacy-focused solutions
  • Community-driven innovation
  • Lower operational costs for businesses

We believe technology should be open — for everyone.
